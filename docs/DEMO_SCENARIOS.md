# Arayanibul MVP - Demo Scenarios

## Overview

This document provides detailed demo scenarios for showcasing the Arayanibul MVP to stakeholders, investors, and potential users. Each scenario demonstrates key features and user journeys in a compelling way.

## Demo Environment Setup

### Prerequisites
- Backend API running on `http://localhost:5000`
- Mobile app accessible via Expo development server
- Test data populated using `scripts/prepare-test-data.sh`
- Demo devices/screens prepared

### Demo Data
All scenarios use pre-populated test data with realistic Turkish content and user profiles.

## Demo Scenario 1: Complete Buyer Journey
**Duration**: 5-7 minutes  
**Audience**: General stakeholders, investors  
**Objective**: Show the complete buyer experience from need creation to transaction completion

### Setup
- **Device**: Mobile phone/tablet
- **User**: Fresh registration or `buyer1@test.com / Test123!`
- **Context**: "Imagine you need to buy an iPhone but want to get the best offers from multiple sellers"

### Demo Script

#### Step 1: Registration/Login (1 minute)
**Narrator**: "Let's start by creating an account. Arayanibul offers multiple ways to join..."

1. Open the app
2. Show registration options:
   - Email registration
   - Google Sign-In
   - Facebook Login
   - Guest access
3. Register with email or use test account
4. **Highlight**: "Notice how quick and simple the onboarding is"

#### Step 2: Create a Need (2 minutes)
**Narrator**: "Now, instead of browsing through thousands of listings, I'll post what I'm looking for..."

1. Tap "İhtiyaç Oluştur" (Create Need)
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Title**: "iPhone 13 Pro Arıyorum"
   - **Category**: Electronics
   - **Description**: "Temiz durumda iPhone 13 Pro arıyorum. 128GB veya 256GB tercih ederim."
   - **Budget**: 20,000 - 25,000 TL
   - **Location**: Enable location or enter manually
   - **Urgency**: Normal
3. Add photos (optional)
4. Submit the need
5. **Highlight**: "The need is now live and visible to all providers in the area"

#### Step 3: Receive and Review Offers (2 minutes)
**Narrator**: "Within minutes, sellers start responding with their best offers..."

1. Navigate to "Tekliflerim" (My Offers)
2. Show received offers (use pre-populated data):
   - Offer 1: 22,000 TL - "Sıfır kutusunda iPhone 13 Pro 128GB"
   - Offer 2: 24,000 TL - "iPhone 13 Pro 256GB, 6 ay garantisi"
3. Compare offers by:
   - Price
   - Description
   - Delivery time
   - Provider rating
4. View provider profiles
5. **Highlight**: "Buyers can easily compare all offers in one place"

#### Step 4: Communication and Acceptance (1-2 minutes)
**Narrator**: "I can message providers directly to ask questions before deciding..."

1. Start conversation with preferred provider
2. Send message: "Merhaba, telefon hangi renk?"
3. Show real-time response (pre-scripted)
4. Accept the best offer
5. **Highlight**: "Once accepted, other offers are automatically declined"

#### Step 5: Wrap-up
**Narrator**: "That's it! The buyer found exactly what they wanted with minimal effort, and sellers competed to offer their best deals."

**Key Points to Emphasize**:
- ✅ Reverse marketplace concept
- ✅ Time-saving for buyers
- ✅ Competitive pricing
- ✅ Direct communication
- ✅ Mobile-first experience

## Demo Tips and Best Practices

### Before the Demo
1. **Test Everything**: Run through scenarios multiple times
2. **Prepare Backup Data**: Have alternative test accounts ready
3. **Check Network**: Ensure stable internet connection
4. **Charge Devices**: Have fully charged devices and chargers
5. **Practice Timing**: Rehearse to fit within time limits

### During the Demo
1. **Start with Context**: Explain the problem being solved
2. **Use Real Data**: Show realistic Turkish content and prices
3. **Highlight Key Features**: Point out unique value propositions
4. **Handle Questions**: Be prepared for technical and business questions
5. **Show Mobile-First**: Emphasize mobile-native experience

### After the Demo
1. **Gather Feedback**: Ask for specific feedback and questions
2. **Provide Follow-up**: Share additional materials if requested
3. **Schedule Next Steps**: Plan follow-up meetings or trials
4. **Document Insights**: Record feedback for product improvement

### Common Questions and Answers

**Q: How is this different from existing marketplaces?**
A: Traditional marketplaces have sellers posting products hoping buyers will find them. Arayanibul reverses this - buyers post what they need, and sellers compete with their best offers.

**Q: How do you ensure quality and prevent fraud?**
A: We use multiple verification layers: user verification, mutual reviews, in-platform communication, and active moderation.

**Q: What's your revenue model?**
A: We plan multiple revenue streams: transaction fees, premium subscriptions, targeted advertising, and data insights.

**Q: How do you handle disputes?**
A: All communication is tracked within the platform, and we have a dispute resolution process with human moderators.

**Q: Can this scale to other markets?**
A: Absolutely. The reverse marketplace model works for any market where buyers have specific needs and sellers can customize their offers.

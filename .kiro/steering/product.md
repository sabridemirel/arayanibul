# Product Overview

**Arayanibul** is a reverse marketplace mobile platform where buyers post what they're looking for instead of sellers posting what they have.

## Core Concept
- **Traditional**: Sellers post "I'm selling X"
- **Arayanibul**: Buyers post "I'm looking for X"
- Service/product providers respond with offers

## Target Use Cases
- Product searches: "Looking for iPhone 13 Pro, budget 25,000 TL"
- Service requests: "Need house cleaning service, weekly"
- Specialized needs: "Looking for wedding photographer for June 15"

## Key Features
- JWT-based authentication with social login support
- Guest user access
- Reverse listing system (buyers create listings)
- Provider bidding system
- Turkish language interface

## Business Model
Future monetization through premium memberships, commission per offer, promoted listings, and advertising revenue.
